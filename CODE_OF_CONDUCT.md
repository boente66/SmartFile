# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be kind and respectful.
- Assume positive intent.
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Disruptive or abusive conduct.

## Enforcement

Reports can be sent to the project maintainers. Inappropriate behavior may result in removal from the project community.
